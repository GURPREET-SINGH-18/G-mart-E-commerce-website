    Types of Earphones
     

    Wireless  Headphones

    Just like the traditional wired headphones, there are wireless headphones that are available online. But, what separates wireless earbuds from the wired ones is how convenient and hassle-free they are as compared to wired headsets. With these headsets, you don’t have to worry about ensuring that their wires don’t get damaged. That’s because these headphones connect to your phone/tablet or any other compatible smart device using Bluetooth and radio frequencies, among other options. This feature not only helps you indulge in high-quality audio, but it also saves you the trouble of untangling the wires every time you wish to listen to good music. You can easily find some great-quality radio frequency headphones or Bluetooth headphones on online stores.  

    Sports Headphones 
    When was the last time that you went to the gym for a workout and your earphones stayed put in your ears? Never, right? You’re probably starting to think, “How do I ensure that my headphones stay in place even during complex movements?” Aren’t you? Well, you certainly need to try sports earphones. These babies not only offer a snug fit to ensure that you’re pumped up throughout your workout sessions, but they are also sweat-resistant so that their performance isn’t affected by it.   
     
    Travel  Headphones
    Go to anyone who travels frequently and ask him/her, “What’s the one piece of advice that you have for an aspiring traveller?’ And, 9 out of 10 times, that person will probably tell you to travel light. Yep, there’s barely anyone who likes dealing with excess baggage weight, especially if it’s in the form of your favorite headphones. And, travel headphones were invented to help people overcome such problems. These headsets aren’t just lightweight, they are easy to pack too. Slide them in your backpack or wear them around your neck - travel headphones won’t be a burden. 
     
    Extra Bass  Headphones
    Not everyone has an ear for music that has powerful lows. But, if you are a glutton for that kind of music, then extra-bass headphones are something that you really need to consider before making a buying decision. Brands such as Sony are famous for manufacturing this type of headphones. Basically, what these headphones do is digitally increase the low-frequency sounds in the audio that you’re listening to and then maximize it to offer you a punchy sound that every bass lover longs to hear.  
      
    Noise-canceling Headphones  
      
    Be it dealing with a bunch of people who just won’t keep quiet or with the annoying noise coming from your neighborhood, situations like these can be quite annoying. But, let me tell you this - you won’t have to deal with such situations if you had a pair of noise-cancellation headphones. Noise-canceling headsets, in general, reduce/eliminate unwanted ambient sounds, and they do so by using active noise control. This helps you enjoy audio content in great quality without even having to raise the volume significantly. Another great thing about these headsets is that they increase the signal-to-noise ratio when you are in an aviation environment, ensuring that you don’t miss out on important announcements. How cool is that?
